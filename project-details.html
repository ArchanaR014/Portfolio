  • Crime Predictive Model/Tool For Hotspot Mapping on Machine Learning Domain

    • Aim: Establishing and maintaining moral barriers against committing criminal acts. Reducing recruitment to criminal social environments and activities by eliminating or reducing the social and individual causes and processes that lead to criminality.

  • Paper Published: International Journal of Advanced Research in Science, Communication and Technology (IJARSCT), April 2023-May 2023

    Title: Crime Predictive Model/Tool For Hotspot Mapping

    • Authors: Sabin TT (Assistant Professor), Archana R, Lithisha K J, Vikhyath P, Department of Information Science and Engineering Students SJC Institute of Technology, Chikabalapura, India.
    Brief Abstract: “Developed a novel Crime Prediction Model/Tool for Hotspot Mapping that utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze and predict crime patterns in urban areas. Implemented advanced statistical techniques and geospatial analysis to identify high-risk areas and potential crime hotspots based on historical crime data, demographic factors, and environmental variables. The model demonstrated an accuracy rate of 85% in predicting crime occurrences, enabling law enforcement agencies to proactively allocate resources and implement targeted crime prevention strategies.”
